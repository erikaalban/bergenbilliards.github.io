Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to join a league?

League green fees vary from $20-$25 per person, depending on the team size. One time signup fee of $100 per team.

Do I need my own equipment?

While many players bring their own cues, house cues are available for all league players. No equipment purchase is necessary to participate.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up at the front desk, call us at (908) 936-7807, or use the online signup links. Registration closes one week before each league begins.
