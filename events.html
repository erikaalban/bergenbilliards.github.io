2025 USBA National Championship

April 24-27, 2025

Join us for the prestigious 2025 USBA National Three-Cushion Championship at Bergen Billiards. This premier event will feature the top carom billiards players from across the country competing for the national title. Spectator admission is free, and all billiards enthusiasts are welcome to attend this exceptional display of skill and precision.
